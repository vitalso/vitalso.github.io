Why trust on us?

Your benefits at a glance

  • Application in German

    Many visa applications are only available in the local language or in English. 123VISA takes care of the entire application process in German for you. We make sure you understand everything.

  • Documents and Evidence

    Various documents and evidence are required for the application, all of which must be in a specific file format. You simply send us your documents as they are and we will take care of the correct format.

  • No PC, scanner or printer

    You don't have a PC, scanner or printer at hand? No problem, thanks to the WhatsApp connection, you simply send us (mobile phone) photos of the required documents and we will do the rest.

  • No risk - the 123VISA guarantee

    Our services are only due after your visa has been successfully applied for and approved. No Visa - No Payment

  • Insurance

    For some visas and entry permits, proof of special (travel) health insurance is required. We check whether your existing insurance covers all services. Alternatively, you can also book suitable insurance at a reasonable price.

  • Technology and Real People

    Real people work for us and we combine modern technology with personal contact. You can view the status of your application 24/7 in our customer area and follow the application process.
