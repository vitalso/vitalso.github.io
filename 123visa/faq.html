What is 123VISA?

123VISA is your modern application service for electronic travel visas worldwide.
Our goal is to make the process as easy as possible, with us you don't have to send your passport by post, a photo is enough for us. You can track the status of your application online at any time and because we are sure that you will be satisfied with us, you do not have to pay anything in advance.

For which countries can you apply for a visa?

We are constantly expanding our range and adding new countries. You can see which countries you can use our innovative service for at any time in the country selection on the start page.

How is the payment made?

The payment of the application fees of the embassy together with our service fee only takes place AFTER your VISA has been successfully approved, you take no risk and do not have to pay anything in advance.

How can I contact you?

Our goal is to make everything as easy and convenient for you as possible. In addition to the classic contact options such as telephone and email, you can also simply write us a WhatsApp or contact us via Facebook Messenger
