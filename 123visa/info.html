General information on entry with ESTA Visa

What is ESTA?

Since 2008, travelers from Visa Waiver Program (VWP) countries are required to apply for an ESTA online before boarding a flight or ship bound for the United States. Passengers who have not registered for the ESTA risk being denied a flight.

What is checked?

The Electronic Travel Authorization System (ESTA) is a process used to assess the vulnerability of visitors to the United States to determine if they pose a risk to homeland security or law enforcement. The system checks law enforcement databases.

Visa regulations

To apply for your ESTA USA we need the following documents

  • Photo of your passport (1x passport photo in colour)
  • Must be valid for at least 6 months after leaving the USA

Changes from May 02, 2022

Beginning May 2, 2022, electronic ESTA authorization will also replace the I-94W form entry.
