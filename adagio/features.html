Adagio is built by the educators, musicians, and staff behind Musopen.org, dedicated to making music education more accessible, organized, and inspiring for teachers and students alike.

Stay on top of
student progress

Monitor practice habits, give feedback, and keep students motivated with interactive tools to track, engage, and support their musical journey.

  • See how often your students are practicing
  • Like, comment, and share to encourage progress
  • Assign homework, share notes, and set goals
  • Listen to audio recordings of practice sessions

Keep students engaged and progressing

Set clear goals, provide structured feedback, and encourage practice with interactive tools designed to support steady improvement.

  • Set weekly or one-time goals
  • Share practice notes after each lesson
  • Comment on practice sessions for encouragement

Simplify homework management

Easily create, track, and organize assignments while keeping students
accountable with automated reminders and bulk messaging.

  • Create assignments for one or multiple students at a time
  • See the status of assignments for all students at a glance
  • Group students to easily message or assign homework in bulk
  • Automatically remind students to complete assignments

Lesson management made musical

Billing and Payments

Calendar
